Expenses Claim Form Print and submit for claims


Finance Claim Procedure

1) Log in to myisis (myisis.nus.edu.sg). Click ‘Self Service’, click ‘Campus Finances’, click ‘Maintain Bank Accounts’ and fill in your ‘Credit Bank Account Details’ accurately.
2) Check that the prints on the receipts are still legible.
3) If the amount to be claimed includes GST, check that the receipt displays the GST Registration Number of the supplier.
4) Paste your receipts on A4 plain papers securely with glue. One receipt is to be pasted on each side of an A4 paper only. Do not use staplers or tapes.
5) Strike out, with the aid of a ruler, any personal items which you have purchased and their amounts on the receipts.
6) Download the Request for Payment (RFP) form below.
7) Fill in the form, except the Signature of Payee, paying special attention to the following:
  • NUS Student ID – Both alphabets at the front and back are to be included
  • Payee’s Name – Fill in your name as stated in your matriculation card.
  • Amount – Amounts of items purchased for the purposes of RC4 from different receipts are to be totaled accurately. Ensure that the decimal point is clearly seen
  • Purpose – Be specific. Include the date of event, if any
In the event of an error, do not use correction fluid or tape. Instead, with the aid of a ruler, strike out the error and countersign against it.
8) Print the form and sign it. Attach the receipts to the form.
9) Pass the completed form and receipts to a Finance Committee member. Allow several weeks for the claim to be processed.
