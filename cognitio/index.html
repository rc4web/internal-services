Cognitio is a cluster for informal learning platforms in RC4, which constitutes many special academic interest groups and specially organized intellectual dialogue sessions (in the form of tea sessions, workshops, and colloquium/symposium). This off-curriculum cluster intends to help our students to expand their understanding of the real world and to sharpen their life skills. The special academic groups and dialogue sessions in this cluster create environments and experiences that empower students to discover and construct knowledge, meaning and purpose for themselves.
